EL MAR Surfboards Inc

El MAR Surfboards founded in 2023 by Yehonatan Malki, Yaniv Rabin, Tal Avraham, Roy Toledo,
is a high performance surfboard brand used by the world's elite surfers.

The range is focused on performance in a range of wave conditions and includes research, design and feedback
 from a range of team riders and athletes in conditions around the world to give you surfboards ready to take on
 any type of wave in all conditions!
